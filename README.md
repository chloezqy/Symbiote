# Symbiote

A living creature sustained by your voice.

Speak to keep it alive. Stay silent, and watch it fall apart.

---

## Concept

Symbiote is an audio-reactive organism that exists in a fragile balance with the user.

The app visualizes sound by modeling a living system whose stability depends on continuous external input. Voice acts as a sustaining force for the creature. When sound disappears, the system drifts.

The project explores a form of interaction where control gradually slips into instability.

---

## Interaction

* **Speak**
  — The organism stabilizes, breathes rhythmically, and appears coherent

* **Vary your voice**
  — Subtle instabilities emerge (asymmetry, tension, fluctuation)

* **Stay silent**
  — The system begins to drift, desynchronize, and degrade

---

## Visual System

* **Surface deformation**
  — Soft-body motion simulating breath and structural instability

* **Internal flow**
  — Directional energy circulation that shifts as the system destabilizes

* **Texture growth**
  — Frequency-driven patterns that emerge, mutate, and decay over time

* **Facial expressions**
  — Embedded within the form, making the system emotionally legible and alive
